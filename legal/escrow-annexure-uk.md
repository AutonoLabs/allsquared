## 4. RELEASE CONDITIONS AND PROCESS

### 4.1 Release Triggers

Escrow Funds shall be released to the Supplier only upon the occurrence of one or more of the following:

(a) **Acceptance Event**: The Client issues written acceptance (or deemed acceptance occurs) of the relevant Deliverable or Milestone;

(b) **Auto-Release**: The Client fails to respond to a Release Request within the Auto-Release Period (see clause 4.4);

(c) **Dispute Resolution**: A determination is made in accordance with the dispute resolution procedure in clause 6; or

(d) **Joint Instruction**: Both Parties provide a joint written instruction to the Escrow Agent.

### 4.2 Release Request

(a) Upon completing a Deliverable or Milestone, the Supplier shall submit a Release Request through the Platform, specifying:

   (i) the Deliverable or Milestone completed;

   (ii) the amount to be released;

   (iii) evidence of completion (e.g., delivery confirmation, test results); and

   (iv) any relevant provisions of the Underlying Agreement.

(b) The Escrow Agent shall promptly notify the Client of the Release Request.

### 4.3 Client Response

(a) The Client shall, within **[CLIENT_RESPONSE_PERIOD]** Business Days of receiving notification of a Release Request:

   (i) **approve** the release (in whole or in part);

   (ii) **reject** the release, providing written reasons and specifying the deficiencies; or

   (iii) **raise a Dispute** in accordance with clause 6.

(b) Partial approval: The Client may approve release of part of the requested amount and dispute the remainder.

### 4.4 Auto-Release

(a) If the Client fails to respond to a Release Request within **[AUTO_RELEASE_PERIOD]** Business Days (the "**Auto-Release Period**"), the Escrow Agent shall release the requested funds to the Supplier automatically.

(b) The Escrow Agent shall send the Client a reminder at the midpoint and **[AUTO_RELEASE_FINAL_REMINDER]** Business Days before the expiry of the Auto-Release Period.

(c) Auto-Release shall not apply where the Client has initiated a Dispute prior to the expiry of the Auto-Release Period.

### 4.5 Release Execution

(a) Upon a Release Trigger occurring, the Escrow Agent shall release the applicable funds to the Supplier Account within **[RELEASE_EXECUTION_PERIOD]** Business Days.

(b) The Escrow Agent shall deduct the Escrow Fee (if not separately invoiced) before releasing funds.

(c) The Escrow Agent shall provide both Parties with a release confirmation, including the amount released, any deductions, and the remaining Escrow balance.

## 5. REFUNDS TO CLIENT

### 5.1 Refund Triggers

Escrow Funds (or a portion thereof) shall be returned to the Client upon:

(a) **Termination**: The Underlying Agreement is terminated (in whole or in part) and the Client is entitled to a refund of unearned Fees;

(b) **Failed Deliverable**: A Deliverable is rejected following the exhaustion of re-submission attempts under the Underlying Agreement;

(c) **Dispute Resolution**: A determination under clause 6 directs a refund;

(d) **Mutual Agreement**: Both Parties agree in writing to a partial or full refund; or

(e) **Non-Commencement**: The Supplier fails to commence Services within the period specified in the Underlying Agreement.

### 5.2 Refund Process

(a) The requesting Party shall submit a refund request through the Platform, with supporting evidence.

(b) The other Party shall be notified and given **[REFUND_RESPONSE_PERIOD]** Business Days to respond.

(c) If approved (or not contested), refunds shall be processed within **[REFUND_PROCESSING_PERIOD]** Business Days.

(d) Disputed refunds shall be handled under clause 6.

## 6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

### 6.1 Platform Mediation

(a) If the Client rejects a Release Request or the Supplier contests a refund, either Party may raise a Dispute through the Platform.

(b) The Escrow Agent shall hold the disputed funds pending resolution.

(c) The Platform shall appoint a dispute resolution specialist who shall:

   (i) review the Underlying Agreement, the Release Request/refund request, and submissions from both Parties;

   (ii) request additional information or evidence from either Party;

   (iii) attempt to facilitate a resolution between the Parties; and

   (iv) if no agreement is reached, make a non-binding recommendation within **[MEDIATION_RECOMMENDATION_PERIOD]** Business Days.

### 6.2 Formal Mediation

If the Dispute is not resolved under clause 6.1, the Parties shall submit to mediation in accordance with the CEDR Model Mediation Procedure, at the Parties' shared cost.

### 6.3 Court Proceedings

(a) If the Dispute remains unresolved after mediation (or if mediation is not completed within **[MEDIATION_DEADLINE]** days), either Party may commence proceedings in the courts of **England and Wales**.

(b) The Escrow Agent shall release the disputed funds in accordance with any court order or settlement agreement.

### 6.4 Interim Measures

(a) During a Dispute, undisputed portions of the Escrow Funds may be released (e.g., for accepted Milestones unrelated to the Dispute).

(b) The Escrow Agent may seek directions from the court if it receives conflicting instructions.

### 6.5 Escrow Agent's Role in Disputes

(a) The Escrow Agent acts as a neutral stakeholder and does not adjudicate Disputes.

(b) The Escrow Agent shall comply with court orders and Dispute resolution outcomes.

(c) The Escrow Agent shall not be liable for any loss arising from the delayed release of funds during a bona fide Dispute.

## 7. OBLIGATIONS OF THE ESCROW AGENT

### 7.1 Duties

The Escrow Agent shall:

(a) hold the Escrow Funds in a segregated client account with an FCA-authorised institution;

(b) maintain accurate records of all deposits, releases, and balances;

(c) release funds only in accordance with this Annexure;

(d) notify both Parties promptly of all deposits, release requests, releases, refunds, and disputes;

(e) provide both Parties with periodic statements (monthly or on request);

(f) comply with all Applicable Law, including anti-money laundering regulations; and

(g) act in good faith and with reasonable care.

### 7.2 No Investment

The Escrow Agent shall not invest the Escrow Funds in any securities, derivatives, or other financial instruments (except interest-bearing deposit accounts as described in clause 2.3).

### 7.3 Anti-Money Laundering

(a) The Escrow Agent shall conduct customer due diligence on both Parties in accordance with the Money Laundering, Terrorist Financing and Transfer of Funds (Information on the Payer) Regulations 2017.

(b) If the Escrow Agent is unable to complete satisfactory due diligence, it may refuse to accept funds or suspend the escrow arrangement.

## 8. LIABILITY OF THE ESCROW AGENT

### 8.1 Standard of Care

The Escrow Agent shall exercise reasonable care in performing its obligations under this Annexure.

### 8.2 Limitation

(a) The Escrow Agent's total aggregate liability under this Annexure shall not exceed the total Escrow Fees received in connection with the relevant engagement.

(b) The Escrow Agent shall not be liable for:

   (i) any delay or failure caused by circumstances beyond its reasonable control;

   (ii) acting in accordance with a court order or the outcome of a Dispute resolution process;

   (iii) the performance or non-performance of the Services by the Supplier;

   (iv) the quality, completeness, or suitability of any Deliverables;

   (v) any loss arising from the insolvency of the bank holding the Escrow Account (to the extent covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme); or

   (vi) indirect or consequential losses.

### 8.3 Indemnity

The Client and Supplier shall jointly and severally indemnify the Escrow Agent against all claims, losses, and costs arising from:

(a) any breach of this Annexure by either of them;

(b) any inaccurate or misleading information provided by either of them; and

(c) any third-party claim related to the Escrow Funds (other than claims arising from the Escrow Agent's negligence or wilful default).

### 8.4 Unlimited Liability

Nothing in this clause 8 limits the Escrow Agent's liability for fraud, wilful default, or death or personal injury caused by negligence.

## 10. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 10.1 Term

This Annexure shall remain in effect until:

(a) all Escrow Funds have been released or refunded;

(b) the Underlying Agreement has been completed or terminated and all payment obligations resolved; or

(c) the Parties and the Escrow Agent agree in writing to terminate the escrow arrangement.

### 10.2 Termination by Escrow Agent

(a) The Escrow Agent may terminate this Annexure by giving **[ESCROW_TERMINATION_NOTICE]** days' written notice to both Parties if:

   (i) it ceases to operate the Platform;

   (ii) it is required to do so by a regulatory authority; or

   (iii) either Party is in material breach of this Annexure.

(b) Upon termination by the Escrow Agent, the Escrow Funds shall be dealt with as follows:

   (i) if the Parties agree on distribution, the Escrow Agent shall release funds accordingly;

   (ii) if the Parties do not agree, the Escrow Agent shall transfer the Escrow Funds to a successor escrow agent agreed by the Parties, or pay the funds into court.
