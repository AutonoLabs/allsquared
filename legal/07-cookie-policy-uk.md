## 1. Introduction

1.1 This Cookie Policy explains how AllSquared Ltd ("AllSquared", "we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website at allsquared.io and all associated subdomains (together, the "Site").

1.3 We use cookies in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018, and the Privacy and Electronic Communications (EC Directive) Regulations 2003 (PECR).

1.4 By continuing to use the Site, you acknowledge that you have read this Cookie Policy. Where cookies require your consent, we will ask for it through our cookie consent banner before placing those cookies on your device.

## 2. What Are Cookies

2.1 Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, phone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, to improve efficiency, and to provide reporting information to website operators.

2.2 **First-party cookies** are set by the website you are visiting (in this case, allsquared.io). **Third-party cookies** are set by a domain other than the one you are visiting, typically by service providers whose technology is integrated into our Site.

2.3 **Session cookies** are temporary and are deleted from your device when you close your browser. **Persistent cookies** remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them manually.

2.4 Cookies may also include similar technologies such as pixels, local storage, and session storage, which function in a comparable way. References to "cookies" in this policy include these similar technologies.

## 3. How We Use Cookies

3.1 We use cookies for the following purposes:

(a) **Essential Function:** To enable core functionality of the Site, including page navigation, access to secure areas, and form submissions. The Site cannot function properly without these cookies.

(b) **Authentication:** To recognise you when you log in to your AllSquared account and to maintain your session while you use the platform.

(c) **Security:** To protect your account and the Site from fraudulent activity, cross-site request forgery, and other security threats.

(d) **Analytics and Performance:** To understand how visitors interact with the Site, which pages are most and least popular, and how visitors navigate the Site. This information helps us improve the Site and your experience.

(e) **Preferences:** To remember choices you make (such as language or display preferences) and to provide a more personalised experience.

(f) **Fraud Detection:** To detect and prevent fraudulent payment activity in connection with our escrow and payment processing services.

(a) **Google Chrome:** [https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)

(b) **Mozilla Firefox:** [https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enhanced-tracking-protection-firefox-desktop](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enhanced-tracking-protection-firefox-desktop)

(c) **Apple Safari:** [https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac](https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac)

## 6. Consent

6.1 Under PECR, we are required to obtain your consent before placing cookies on your device, except where those cookies are strictly necessary for the provision of a service you have requested (essential cookies).

6.2 When you first visit the Site, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner. This banner provides you with clear information about the categories of cookies we use and allows you to give or refuse consent for each category separately.

6.3 Essential cookies (as identified in the table at clause 4) are exempt from the consent requirement under Regulation 6(4) of PECR because they are strictly necessary to provide the service you have requested. These cookies are placed regardless of your consent choices.

6.4 Analytics, functional, and marketing cookies are only placed on your device if you give your affirmative consent through the cookie consent banner or the cookie preference centre.

6.5 You may withdraw your consent at any time by:

(a) visiting the AllSquared cookie preference centre at [https://allsquared.io/cookie-preferences](https://allsquared.io/cookie-preferences);

(b) adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies; or

(c) contacting us at privacy@allsquared.io.

6.6 Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out on the basis of consent before its withdrawal.
