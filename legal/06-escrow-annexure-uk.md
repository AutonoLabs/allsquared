## 1. Purpose and Incorporation

1.1 This Escrow Annexure ("Annexure") forms part of the service contract identified by the Contract Reference above (the "Principal Agreement") between the Client and the Freelancer, facilitated through the AllSquared platform.

1.2 The purpose of this Annexure is to set out the terms on which AllSquared acts as Escrow Agent in respect of Milestone Payments made by the Client in connection with the Project.

1.3 In the event of any conflict or inconsistency between the terms of the Principal Agreement and the terms of this Annexure, this Annexure prevails to the extent that the conflict or inconsistency relates to escrow matters.

1.4 AllSquared Ltd acts as Escrow Agent in accordance with the terms set out in this Annexure and the AllSquared Terms of Service. AllSquared does not act as a party to the Principal Agreement other than in its capacity as Escrow Agent.

## 2. Definitions

In this Annexure, unless the context requires otherwise:

**"Acceptance Criteria"** means the specific requirements, standards, or deliverables that a Milestone submission must satisfy before the Client is required to approve release of the corresponding Milestone Payment, as set out in the Milestone Payment Schedule at clause 3.

**"Acceptance Period"** means a period of 5 Working Days commencing on the Working Day after AllSquared notifies the Client that the Freelancer has submitted a Milestone for review.

**"Deliverable"** means any work product, output, or service delivered by the Freelancer in fulfilment of a Milestone.

**"Dispute Notice"** means a written notice submitted by the Client to AllSquared through the AllSquared platform during the Acceptance Period, setting out a specific and reasoned objection as to why a Milestone submission does not meet the applicable Acceptance Criteria.

**"Escrow Account"** means the designated client account held by AllSquared with an authorised financial institution, into which Escrow Funds are deposited and held in accordance with this Annexure.

**"Escrow Agent"** means AllSquared Ltd, acting in its capacity as escrow agent under this Annexure.

**"Escrow Funds"** means all sums deposited by the Client into the Escrow Account in connection with the Milestone Payments under this Annexure.

**"Freeze Period"** means the period during which Escrow Funds attributable to a disputed Milestone are frozen pending resolution in accordance with clause 5.

**"Milestone"** means each discrete phase of work described in the Milestone Payment Schedule at clause 3, completion of which triggers the Freelancer's entitlement to the corresponding Milestone Payment (subject to the terms of this Annexure).

**"Milestone Payment"** means the sum payable in respect of each Milestone, as set out in the Milestone Payment Schedule at clause 3.

**"Release Condition"** means the condition that must be satisfied before AllSquared releases a Milestone Payment to the Freelancer, being either: (a) express written approval by the Client; or (b) expiry of the Acceptance Period without the Client raising a valid Dispute Notice.

**"Working Day"** means any day other than a Saturday, Sunday, or public holiday in England and Wales.

## 4. Escrow Mechanics

### 4.1 Funding

(a) The Client must deposit the Milestone Payment for a given Milestone into the Escrow Account before the Freelancer is required to commence work on that Milestone.

(b) AllSquared must confirm receipt of each Milestone Payment to both the Client and the Freelancer within 1 Working Day of the funds clearing in the Escrow Account.

(c) The Freelancer is under no obligation to commence or continue work on a Milestone until AllSquared has confirmed that the corresponding Milestone Payment has been received and is held in the Escrow Account.

(d) If the Client fails to deposit the Milestone Payment by the applicable Due Date, the Freelancer may, at the Freelancer's sole discretion, suspend performance of the relevant Milestone until such time as the deposit is made.

### 4.2 Freelancer Submission

(a) On completing a Milestone, the Freelancer must submit the relevant Deliverable through the AllSquared platform.

(b) The Freelancer must include a written submission notice confirming that the Deliverable has been completed in accordance with the applicable Acceptance Criteria.

(c) AllSquared must notify the Client of the Freelancer's submission within 1 Working Day. The Acceptance Period commences on the Working Day following that notification.

### 4.3 Acceptance Period

(a) The Client has 5 Working Days from the start of the Acceptance Period to either:

    (i) approve the release of the Milestone Payment to the Freelancer; or

    (ii) raise a Dispute Notice setting out specific written objections as to why the Deliverable does not satisfy the Acceptance Criteria.

(b) A Dispute Notice that does not contain specific and reasoned objections referencing the applicable Acceptance Criteria is not a valid Dispute Notice. AllSquared may, at its discretion, reject a Dispute Notice that is vague, generic, or does not reference the Acceptance Criteria.

### 4.4 Automatic Release

(a) If the Client does not approve the release or raise a valid Dispute Notice within the Acceptance Period, the Milestone Payment is automatically deemed approved.

(b) On automatic deemed approval, AllSquared must release the Milestone Payment to the Freelancer (less the AllSquared Service Fee) within 2 Working Days.

### 4.5 Approved Release

(a) On the Client expressly approving the release of a Milestone Payment, AllSquared must release the funds to the Freelancer (less the AllSquared Service Fee) within 2 Working Days of receiving that approval.

(b) AllSquared must provide both parties with written confirmation of each release.

### 4.6 Dispute Freeze

(a) On receipt of a valid Dispute Notice, AllSquared must immediately freeze the Escrow Funds attributable to the disputed Milestone.

(b) Frozen funds remain in the Escrow Account and must not be released to either party until the dispute is resolved in accordance with clause 5.

(c) AllSquared must notify the Freelancer of the Dispute Notice and provide a copy of the Client's written objections within 1 Working Day of receipt.

### 4.7 AllSquared Service Fee

(a) AllSquared is entitled to deduct a service fee of 10% of each Milestone Payment at the point of release to the Freelancer (the "Service Fee").

(b) The Service Fee is deducted from the Milestone Payment amount. The Freelancer receives the Milestone Payment less the Service Fee.

(c) Both the Client and the Freelancer acknowledge and agree to the Service Fee as a condition of using the AllSquared escrow service. The Service Fee is non-refundable once deducted, except where AllSquared determines that a deduction was made in error.

## 5. Dispute Resolution

5.1 On receipt of a valid Dispute Notice, AllSquared must notify both the Client and the Freelancer within 1 Working Day and initiate the dispute review process set out in this clause 5.

5.2 The Client and the Freelancer have 10 Working Days from the date of AllSquared's notification under clause 5.1 to attempt to resolve the dispute directly. During this period, either party may submit evidence and written representations to AllSquared through the platform.

5.3 If the dispute is not resolved within the 10 Working Day period under clause 5.2, AllSquared must appoint an independent reviewer with relevant expertise to determine the dispute. The independent reviewer must issue a written determination within 15 Working Days of appointment. The determination is binding on both the Client and the Freelancer, subject to clause 5.5.

5.4 AllSquared may, on the basis of the independent reviewer's determination or on the basis of evidence submitted by the parties, release all or part of the frozen Escrow Funds to the Client, the Freelancer, or both, in proportion to the work completed and accepted against the applicable Acceptance Criteria.

5.5 Either party may appeal the independent reviewer's determination to the courts of England and Wales within 30 calendar days of the date of the determination. Pending any such appeal, the Escrow Funds remain frozen unless the court orders otherwise.

5.6 AllSquared is not liable for the outcome of any determination made under this clause 5 unless the determination is made in bad faith or as a result of AllSquared's wilful misconduct.

## 6. Refund Conditions

6.1 The Client is entitled to a full refund of Escrow Funds attributable to an incomplete Milestone in the following circumstances:

(a) the Freelancer fails to submit the Deliverable by the applicable Due Date and does not remedy the failure within 14 calendar days of receiving written notice from the Client requesting cure;

(b) the Freelancer abandons the Project, which for these purposes means the Freelancer expressly communicates an intention to cease work or fails to respond to communications through the AllSquared platform for a continuous period of 14 calendar days; or

(c) there is evidence of material fraud by the Freelancer in connection with the Milestone or the Project.

6.2 Where the Client is entitled to a partial refund, the refund amount must be calculated in proportion to the Deliverables that have not been accepted by the Client against the applicable Acceptance Criteria. Accepted Deliverables are not subject to refund.

6.3 AllSquared must process any refund within 5 Working Days of determining that the refund conditions in this clause 6 have been satisfied.

## 7. AllSquared's Role as Escrow Agent

7.1 AllSquared acts as Escrow Agent in an administrative capacity only. AllSquared is not a trustee (except to the limited extent set out in clause 9) and does not owe fiduciary duties to either the Client or the Freelancer in its capacity as Escrow Agent.

7.2 AllSquared's obligation to hold and release Escrow Funds is contractual and arises solely under this Annexure and the AllSquared Terms of Service.

7.3 AllSquared may deduct the Service Fee and any other verified fees or charges properly due under this Annexure or the AllSquared Terms of Service before releasing Escrow Funds.

7.4 If AllSquared is unable to determine the rightful recipient of Escrow Funds after following the procedures set out in this Annexure (including clause 5), AllSquared may interplead to the courts of England and Wales and deposit the disputed funds with the court. AllSquared's liability in respect of those funds ceases on such deposit.

7.5 AllSquared is not liable for any delay in release of Escrow Funds caused by banking processing times, technical failures outside AllSquared's reasonable control, or the Client's or Freelancer's failure to provide required payment information.

## 8. Interest

8.1 Escrow Funds are held in a pooled client account. No interest is payable to the Client or the Freelancer on Escrow Funds held in the Escrow Account.

8.2 AllSquared may earn interest on Escrow Funds held in the pooled account. This arrangement is disclosed in the AllSquared Terms of Service, and both the Client and the Freelancer accept this arrangement by agreeing to this Annexure.

## 9. Insolvency Protection

9.1 Escrow Funds held in the Escrow Account are held on trust by AllSquared for the beneficial owners of those funds. The beneficial owner of each Milestone Payment is the Client until such time as the funds are released to the Freelancer in accordance with this Annexure, at which point beneficial ownership passes to the Freelancer.

9.2 Escrow Funds do not form part of AllSquared's own assets and are not available to AllSquared's creditors in the event of AllSquared's insolvency.

9.3 In the event of AllSquared's insolvency, the Client and the Freelancer may recover Escrow Funds through the applicable insolvency process. AllSquared must maintain adequate records to identify the beneficial ownership of all Escrow Funds at all times.
