# AllSquared Brand Concepts

Three distinct brand directions for AllSquared — the UK's freelance contract and escrow marketplace.

---

## Concept A — Trust & Security

*Clean, professional, banking-grade feel. Think Stripe, Wise, Mercury.*

### Brand Foundations

- **Purpose:** AllSquared makes freelance payments safe, predictable, and legally sound.
- **Core Values:** Reliability, Transparency, Protection
- **Brand Personality:** Serious, formal, confident, modern, understated
- **Voice:** Authoritative, precise, reassuring

### Visual Identity

| Token | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Primary** | Navy Blue `#0F172A` |
| **Primary Light** | `#1E293B` |
| **Secondary** | Slate `#475569` |
| **Accent** | Emerald `#10B981` (success/money) |
| **Surface** | `#F8FAFC` |
| **Border** | `#E2E8F0` |

- **Heading Font:** DM Sans (700, 600) — geometric, clean, trustworthy
- **Body Font:** Inter (400, 500) — highly legible, neutral
- **Logo Concept:** The wordmark "AllSquared" in DM Sans Bold with the "²" superscript rendered as a small rounded square icon — referencing both "squared" mathematically and "squaring up" (settling accounts). The mark alone is the superscript square with a subtle checkmark embedded in its corner. Monochrome navy, no gradients.
- **Pattern/Texture:** Fine dot grids and thin ruled lines — evokes ledger paper and precision.

### Taglines

- **Primary:** "Every deal, squared away."
- **Secondary:**
  - "Contracts you can trust. Payments you can count on."
  - "The safe way to freelance."

---

## Concept B — Modern Creative

*Bold, energetic, design-forward. Think Figma, Linear, Notion.*

### Brand Foundations

- **Purpose:** AllSquared gives freelancers and clients a beautifully simple way to work together with total confidence.
- **Core Values:** Clarity, Momentum, Craft
- **Brand Personality:** Playful-serious, casual, confident, modern, bold
- **Voice:** Direct, energetic, sharp

### Visual Identity

| Token | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Primary** | Electric Violet `#7C3AED` |
| **Primary Light** | `#8B5CF6` |
| **Secondary** | Deep Indigo `#312E81` |
| **Accent** | Hot Pink `#EC4899` |
| **Accent Alt** | Cyan `#06B6D4` |
| **Surface** | `#FAFAFA` |
| **Surface Tint** | `#F5F3FF` (violet-50) |
| **Border** | `#E5E7EB` |

- **Heading Font:** Space Grotesk (700, 600) — geometric, distinctive, technical but warm
- **Body Font:** Inter (400, 500) — crisp, modern, pairs perfectly
- **Logo Concept:** "allsquared" in Space Grotesk Bold, all lowercase — approachable and modern. The "a" and "d" bookend letters are slightly customized with squared-off counters. The standalone mark is two overlapping rounded squares rotated 12° from each other — one violet, one pink — creating a dynamic intersection that represents two parties coming together. Gradient-capable for digital, solid violet for print.
- **Pattern/Texture:** Geometric mesh gradients with violet-to-pink-to-cyan flows. Subtle noise texture overlays on surfaces. Angular decorative shapes (rotated squares, diagonal lines).

### Taglines

- **Primary:** "Freelance, fairly."
- **Secondary:**
  - "Ship work. Get paid. No drama."
  - "The platform that squares things up."

---

## Concept C — Warm & Accessible

*Friendly, approachable, human. Think Gusto, Calendly, Loom.*

### Brand Foundations

- **Purpose:** AllSquared takes the stress out of freelance payments so people can focus on doing great work.
- **Core Values:** Simplicity, Fairness, Care
- **Brand Personality:** Playful, casual, warm, modern, friendly
- **Voice:** Conversational, encouraging, clear

### Visual Identity

| Token | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Primary** | Warm Coral `#F97066` |
| **Primary Light** | `#FCA5A1` |
| **Secondary** | Soft Teal `#2DD4BF` |
| **Accent** | Sunny Yellow `#FBBF24` |
| **Surface** | `#FFFBF5` (warm white) |
| **Text** | `#1C1917` (stone-900) |
| **Border** | `#E7E5E4` (stone-200) |

- **Heading Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans (700, 600) — rounded, friendly, modern
- **Body Font:** Inter (400, 500) — clean, readable
- **Logo Concept:** "AllSquared" in Plus Jakarta Sans Bold with soft letter-spacing. The standalone mark is a rounded square with a friendly smile curve at the bottom and two dots for eyes — a face made from geometric shapes, evoking a satisfied emoji. The square shape = "squared", the smile = satisfaction of a fair deal. Coral primary with teal secondary accent on the smile.
- **Pattern/Texture:** Soft organic blobs, rounded shapes, subtle grain textures. Warm gradient backgrounds from coral to peach to cream.

### Taglines

- **Primary:** "Work happy. Pay happy."
- **Secondary:**
  - "Fair deals for real people."
  - "The friendly way to freelance."

---

## Recommendation

**Implement Concept B (Modern Creative)** for the following reasons:

1. **Differentiation** — Most fintech/contract platforms use Concept A (safe navy). Concept B stands out in the market while still feeling professional.
2. **Target audience** — UK freelancers and creative professionals will resonate with a design-forward brand more than a corporate banking aesthetic.
3. **Versatility** — The violet-pink-cyan palette gives us rich gradient options for data visualization, status indicators, and marketing materials.
4. **Memorability** — The bold color palette and geometric identity are immediately recognizable and work well at all sizes.
