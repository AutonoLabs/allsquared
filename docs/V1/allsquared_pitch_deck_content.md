### Slide 6: FCA-Backed Escrow and Milestone Management

**Content:**

**Escrow Integration:**

**Key Benefit:** Eliminates payment risk for providers and quality risk for clients.

**Visual:** Screenshot or mockup of milestone tracking dashboard.

**Speaker Notes:**
AllSquared partners with FCA-authorised escrow providers to ensure complete payment security. This protects both parties: providers are assured of payment, and clients are assured of quality. Our milestone management system makes this process transparent and automatic.
